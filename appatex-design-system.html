Usage Guidelines

Typography Hierarchy

Use Playfair Display for headlines, section titles, and brand elements. Use Inter for all body text, navigation, buttons, and detailed information. Maintain consistent font weights and sizing across similar elements.

Color Application

Primary dark (#1a1a1a) for main text and professional elements. Gold accent (#d4a574) sparingly for highlights, CTAs, and luxury touches. Maintain high contrast ratios for accessibility.

Voice Consistency

Always lead with expertise and results. Use specific numbers and achievements. Balance professional authority with approachability. Avoid industry jargon when speaking to potential clients.

Visual Balance

Combine clean, modern layouts with sophisticated typography. Use ample white space to convey premium positioning. Include high-quality imagery that showcases textile craftsmanship and global operations.
